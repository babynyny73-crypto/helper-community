1. Project Idea

App Description

ButterflyConnect is a vibrant, interactive app designed for nature lovers, students, and citizen scientists to explore the fascinating world of butterflies. Whether you're spotting Monarchs in your backyard or learning about endangered species across the globe, ButterflyConnect brings the magic of these winged wonders to your fingertips.

Target Audience

Students & Educators Teachers can use the app as a learning tool in biology, environmental science, or ecology classes. Students gain hands-on experience with species identification and conservation.

Value Proposition

Users can join local butterfly walks, workshops, or conservation events through the app. A social feed lets users post sightings, photos, and tips, building a vibrant community of nature enthusiasts. People feel more connected to their environment when they know their observations matter. Your app turns passive interest into active stewardship.
